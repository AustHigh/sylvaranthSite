BOLLOCK: THE HALFLING DRUNKEN BRAWLER

The only thing more painful than Bollock's alcoholism is his right hook. His left hook is pretty painful too. Bollock always seems to find himself in trouble, whether it be through drunken rambling or otherwise. However, Bollock is more than capable of handling himself in a fight, as he is able to empower his fists (which already pack quite a punch) with Ki to make them even more effective, or to dish out faster hits.

Bollock grew up in vast wealth, which his parents shared with him through a monthly allowance. He used this monthly allowance to constantly go out and drink after his schooling. After his parents found out, they cut off his allowance. In order to afford his alcoholism, Bollock began starting barfights to earn money. After drinking so much, Bollock began not being hungover the night after. The alcohol barely did anything for him anymore. However, this just caused him to drink more, to chase the feeling it used to give him. In order to earn more money to further fuel his alcoholism, he began adventuring...



KALGAD: THE GOLIATH BARBARIAN

Being a mountain-dwelling descendant of giants, Kalgad has an inherently short temper. This is brought to an extreme in the heat of battle. Able to fly into a rage at the drop of a hat, Kalgad uses his immense strength to overpower his opponents, weilding a mighty greataxe. Kalgad has a short patience and a disdain for evil-doers.

Hailing from the Kathumnai mountains in the northern region of Sylvaranth, Kalgad was a well respected member of the Thal-Kathumani clan. However, Kalgad had grown tired of staying secluded within the mountains. He desired more adventure, a more challenging existence. Down the mountain he descended, into the vast and untamed wilderness of Sylvaranth...



ZADKIEL: THE ANGELIC SORCERER

Zadkiel is an Aasimar sorcerer, specializing in evocation and transmutation magic. As an Aasimar, Zadkiel can call upon his angelic spirit to sprout heavenly wings which allow him to fly. Zadkiel is also a helpful soul, always trying to do good in the name of his angelic guardian, Armaita.

Zadkiel goes from town to town helping all those in need. With Armaita guiding him, he cleans the land in a raidant glow. Abandoned at birth and raised by an orphanage, Zadkiel swore to protect the innocent, not only for himself and others, but for the Gods who chose him...
