ADVENTURES IN SYLVARANTH
HOME WORLD HEROES CAMPAIGN LORE
CHAPTER 1: THE MEETING OF HEROES

   Our story begins like most stories begin... in the local tavern. In this tavern there are four notable individuals. Each of them are destined to become heroes that will save this land, although this is not apparent at first. They have a long, long journey ahead of them before this becomes clear. These individuals, a Tabaxi, a Halfling, a Goliath, and an Aasimar. They converse with one another, each of them catching another's attention. The Goliath gets fed up with the Halfling's crass behavior, and leaves prematurely. The Halfling doesn't seem to mind, he's too invested in his night of debauchery and drinking. The Tabaxi's patience also runs thin, leaving soon after the Goliath. The Aasimar sticks around, but only out of guilt for this drunken Halfling. Eventually, the remaining two of note leave the tavern, and destiny once again brings them together when stories of a haunted castle containing a Goliath artifact are brought to the party's attention. After some preparation, our party leaves for this castle, their very first quest.

CHAPTER 2: THE CASTLE OF SHADOWS

   The group approaches the abandoned castle. The most peculiar thing about this castle was the lack of a door. After some heavy investigation, the group discovered some out of place brickwork on one of the castle walls. As it turns out, if the bricks were pressed inwards in a particular order, a doorway is revealed! How conveninet...
As our heroes descend into the darkness of this castle, a feeling of dread washes over them. This castle is haunted, who knows what horrors are waiting for unsuspecting adventurers down here? Our heroes enter the main chamber of the castle, and come face to face with shadowy warriors and shadow hounds! They charge into battle, Kalgad swining his greataxe, Bollock throwing punches left and right, Blade slinking through the battlefield with her scimitar, and Zadkiel utilizing fire spells to dispell the shadows. After a hard-fought battle, our heroes emerge victorious from this encounter. Hopefully the will get a moment of respite in this accursed place...

CHAPTER 3: THE KING OF KHODEL

   As our heroes pull out of the castle, the beginning of their fellowship begins to form. With the castle cleared of apparitions and the Goliath artifact in hand, our heroes make their way back to town. As they come into town, there is a commotion in the town square. The party merges with the crowd and the cause of the commotion becomes clear. King Khodel III himself has made himself known in town. He declares that he himself will clear the nearby abandoned castle of shadows and... wait, didn't our heroes already do that? Learning that the king's declaration has already come to pass, he requests that the party let the townsfolk believe that he is the one responsible for the cleansing of the castle. In exchange, the king recognizes the party's capability, and requests their presence at the castle in the city of Khodel. It's a three day journey, so the party spends the rest of the day preparing for the trip, and heads off towards the city of Khodel. The adventure continues!
