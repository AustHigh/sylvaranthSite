★ KHODEL: A port city ruled by King Khodel III. A budding, cultural city. Filled with artists, writers, and musicians who come from all around to make a living doing what they enjoy.
Full of many works from local artists, the Khodel Institute of Art is well known for its spectacular pieces.
This city is also home to The Docks, full of sailors and merchants. This is always a lively side of town and the source of the city's main imports and exports across the sea.
This city is where our heroes saved King Kohdel from a Doppleganger attack, and earned his respect, earning a place among his royal guard.
